Fun Facts About Cats

Did you know that your cat's sleeping position says a lot about your relationships?

If your cat sleeps by your head, then they think that you're their mom and are ready to be groomed.

If they sleep by your feet, then they think that you're their kitten who needs to be protected at all times.

If they sleep on top of you, then they think of you as one of their belongings and think that you're living in their house rent free.
